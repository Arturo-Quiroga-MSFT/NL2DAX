NL Query: List top 10 counterparties by total exposure USD.
Generated DAX:
Here’s a straight‐forward DAX query that returns the top 10 counterparties by total exposure in USD (descending):  

EVALUATE  
TOPN(  
    10,  
    SUMMARIZE(  
        FIS_TOP_COUNTERPARTIES_ANALYSIS,  
        FIS_TOP_COUNTERPARTIES_ANALYSIS[COUNTERPARTY_NAME],  
        "TotalExposureUSD",  
            SUM( FIS_TOP_COUNTERPARTIES_ANALYSIS[TOTAL_EXPOSURE_USD] )  
    ),  
    [TotalExposureUSD],  
    DESC  
)  
ORDER BY  
    [TotalExposureUSD] DESC  

—  
Explanation of the pieces:  
• SUMMARIZE builds a two-column table:  
  – COUNTERPARTY_NAME  
  – TotalExposureUSD (the SUM of TOTAL_EXPOSURE_USD per counterparty)  
• TOPN(10, …, [TotalExposureUSD], DESC) takes the top 10 rows by that summed exposure.  
• ORDER BY [TotalExposureUSD] DESC explicitly sorts the final result.
